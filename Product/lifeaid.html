LifeAID

A pneumatic, rechargeable, multi-dose auto-injector for rapid deployment

Most auto-injectors rely on spring-loaded, single-use mechanisms, but severe anaphylactic reactions may require repeated dosing within minutes. LifeAID is a pneumatic auto-injector concept with three replaceable cartridges, designed to deliver rapid sequential doses from one compact device when seconds matter.

Additional doses

Symptoms don't always stop after one injection.

Single-use devices

Impact

Keeps care continuous by delivering multiple doses without interruption, allowing responders to stay focused on patients, not devices.

Seamless Sequential Delivery

Instant Response

First dose fires immediately for rapid action.

Cartridge Reload

After each injection, replace the used needle cap. Then turn the Rotary Grip clockwise until it clicks and locks. The tactile and audible feedback confirms the next cartridge is ready.

Fast Priming

After three doses, the actuator resets upward. Pump three times until it locks, then rotate to unlock and LifeAID is ready again.

LifeAID's triple-cylinder system delivers uninterrupted, back-to-back doses, eliminating delays when every second matters.

Form

The cylindrical body efficiently houses three rotating cartridges and keeps each dose aligned to the same injection point. The form is driven by tight internal packaging and consistent external usability.
