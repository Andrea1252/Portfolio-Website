LifeAID

A pneumatic, rechargeable, multi-dose auto-injector for rapid deployment

Most auto-injectors rely on spring-loaded, single-use mechanisms, but severe anaphylactic reactions may require repeated dosing within minutes. LifeAID is a pneumatic auto-injector concept with three replaceable cartridges, designed to deliver rapid sequential doses from one compact device when seconds matter.

Pneumatic Sequential Loading

A pneumatic, triple-cylinder system designed for seamless sequential delivery. After an injection, the user replaces the needle cap and turns the Rotary Grip clockwise until it clicks. This tactile and audible feedback confirms the next cartridge is aligned and ready for deployment.

First Responder & Military CMF

The form is driven by tight internal packaging and external usability. High-visibility orange is utilized for first responders to ensure fast recognition in emergencies, while a low-visibility, durable CMF was developed for military squad leaders who must provide quick medical support to their teams.
