Lululemon Weeding Fork

Brand identity exploration

A design exercise exploring how Lululemon’s lifestyle-focused brand identity could extend beyond apparel into gardening tools. This project reimagines the humble weeding fork as a premium, ergonomic product that reflects the brand’s sleek, performance-driven aesthetic and wellness ethos.

Category Product

Date 2023

Duration 4 Weeks
